Animal Conservation Profile

Lion

Species: Panthera leo

Age: 5 years

Description: The lion is known for its majestic appearance and is a symbol of strength. However, it faces threats from habitat loss and hunting.

Conservation Efforts

Threats: Habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict.

Conservation Status: Vulnerable

How You Can Help

  • Support organizations that protect lion habitats and fight poaching.
  • Raise awareness about the importance of lions in the ecosystem.
  • Contribute to wildlife conservation funds and participate in campaigns.
